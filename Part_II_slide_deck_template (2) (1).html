Investigation Overview¶

The goal is to see those features that affects the total biking duration of each participant in the dataset.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area collected through January 2018 to December 2018

(Visualization 1)¶

The duration of biking is spread over a wide a range of time (in seconds) so a log scale was employed to make a convenient plot. it is observed that a large portion of the participants spend about 1,000s biking.

In [5]:
log_binsize = 0.025
bins = 10 ** np.arange(2.4, np.log10(df['duration_sec'].max())+log_binsize, log_binsize)

#plt.figure(figsize=[10, 8])
plt.hist(data = df, x = 'duration_sec', bins = bins)
plt.title('Biking Duration')
plt.xscale('log')
plt.xlim([250, 2e4])
plt.xlabel('Time spent(in seconds)')
plt.ylabel('Number Of people')
plt.show()
